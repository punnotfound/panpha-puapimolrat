Benefits of Good Sleep

Sleep is not just resting - it's when your body repairs itself, your brain processes memories, and you recharge for the next day.

Why Sleep Matters:

  • Improves memory and learning
  • Boosts immune system
  • Enhances mood and reduces stress
  • Supports physical recovery
  • Increases energy and focus

My Sleep Tips

For Better Sleep:

  • Keep a consistent sleep schedule (even on weekends)
  • Create a dark, quiet, and cool bedroom environment
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime
  • Don't consume caffeine after 2 PM
  • Use comfortable pillows and mattress
  • Try relaxing activities before bed (reading, meditation)
